Common Questions
Click a question to expand the answer. Can't find what you need? Submit a help ticket.
How do I apply for a leave? +
Go to the Common Employee Section and click Leave Application Form. Fill in your details, select the dates, and submit. Your manager will be notified automatically. You should receive a confirmation within 1 working day.
Where do I find my salary slip? +
Salary slips are archived under Accounts Section → Payroll → Salary Slips Archive. If your slip is missing, contact the Accounts team via the extension directory.
How do I request an urgent payment or reimbursement? +
Use the Urgent Payment Form in the Common Employee Section. Include the amount, reason, and attach any supporting invoices. Payments are processed within 2 working days after approval.
What are the delivery timings for Mumbai orders? +
All deliveries are made in the second half of the chosen date — between 3:30 PM and 9:30 PM IST via our personal staff. This is a route-based system. Minimum order value for delivery is ₹1,200. Anything below that requires a pickup from our Saki Naka kitchen.
Who do I contact for IT or portal issues? +
For any issues with this portal, Google Workspace access, or internal tools — please submit a Help Ticket using the form below, or contact the MIS team directly via the extension numbers listed in the Common Employee Section.
Where can I find company policies and the employee handbook? +
All policy documents are in the Common Employee Section → Additional Resources. The Employee Handbook (Mumbai Office) covers HR policies, code of conduct, and kitchen safety protocols.
